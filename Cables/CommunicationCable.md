Communication Cable
=========

![Communication Cable](CommunicationCable.png)  
The Communication Cable is the hardware connection for communication with [Uart WiFi Board](https://github.com/NaoNaoMe/Uart-WiFi-Board).

| 4             | 4             |
| 5             | -             |
